How to make iced coffee
Items needed
Stove top
deep metal pan
ingredients:
coffee mix
2 bottles water
sugar
ice
Steps:
Step 1: put deep metal pan and put on the stove.
Step 2: turn on stove.
Step 3: get water and put in pan.
Step 4: wait until it boils.
Step 5: put coffee mix in boiling water.
Step 6: add 3 table spoons of sugar as well.
Step 7: stir until it all mixes well.
Step 8: grab coffee and put in fridge to cool down.
Step 9: when cooled down, serve some in a cup.
Step 10: put ice in the cup of coffee and enjoy
